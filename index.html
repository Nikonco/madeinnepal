Namaste,

Made in nepal is a documentary project about showing the beauty of Nepal and his people. In september 2015 we will be two french guys travelling over Nepal to listen, share and build stories. After the earthquake that hit Nepal in april 2015 the tourism which is a huge part of the revenue income for the country considerably decrease. However after a first time there last year, in my opinion, this country, those people, deserve to be seen and heard. We will share our experience and hope you will enjoy and why not one day, visit on your own.

We will not be able to share our day by day trip but as soon as we will come back, we will get back to you. Just give us your email and stay tuned!

Many thanks,

Titi & Niko
